ABOUT CENTSRENTS

CentsRents is platform for people who want to put an item on rent or want an item on rent.

Our aim is to build an online platform which connects people over requirements and availability, to set in motion a rent contract. A rent contract is similar to an online purchase in this case, where one of the parties involved searches for an item that they require but don't want to purchase, while the other party comprises of individual(s) possessing this particular item and is in no immediate need of it. The subsequent exchange that takes place between these two parties involves a lending of sorts with an official contract inclusive of a deposit amount and a promissory amount to be later paid.
